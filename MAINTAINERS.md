## Maintainer Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:

- Reviewing issues and pull requests.
- Keeping the project buildable and releasable.
- Maintaining the k2s CLI, PowerShell modules, addon system, packaging workflows, documentation, and tests.
- Protecting the security and integrity of the project.
- Helping contributors understand project scope, architecture, and contribution expectations.
- Making release and roadmap decisions in the public project repository.

## Becoming a Maintainer

Maintainers are added based on sustained, high-quality participation in the project. Signals include:

- Consistent contributions through issues, pull requests, reviews, documentation, testing, or release work.
- Good judgment about project scope, quality, security, and user impact.
- Constructive participation in technical discussions.
- Willingness to help other contributors.

New maintainers are proposed by existing maintainers and added after lazy consensus.

## Emeritus Maintainers

Maintainers who step back from active work may be moved to emeritus status. Emeritus maintainers are recognized for their contributions but are not expected to participate in day-to-day reviews or releases.
